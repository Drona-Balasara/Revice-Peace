For the best experience and proper binaural beat effect, please use headphones. Binaural beats work by playing different frequencies in each ear, which requires stereo separation that only headphones can provide.

Note: Binaural beats may not be suitable for individuals with epilepsy or those prone to seizures. Consult with a healthcare professional if you have concerns.
