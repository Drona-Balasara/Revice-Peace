Meditation Techniques

Discover meditation practices to calm your mind, reduce stress, and find inner peace.

Popular Meditation Techniques

Explore these meditation styles and their unique benefits.

Mindfulness Meditation

Be present in the moment with focused awareness

Mindfulness Meditation encourages you to observe your thoughts, feelings, and sensations without judgment, fostering calm and clarity.

Awareness Calm Non-judgmental Focus

Transcendental Meditation

A simple mantra-based meditation for deep rest

Transcendental Meditation uses a personal mantra, repeated silently, to settle the mind and promote relaxation and self-development.

Mantra Deep Rest Simplicity Self-Development

Guided Meditation

Relax and focus with the help of a guide or teacher

Guided Meditation uses imagery, visualization, or spoken instructions to help you relax, focus, and achieve specific goals.

Guided Visualization Relaxation Focus

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Cultivate compassion and love for yourself and others

Loving-Kindness Meditation (Metta) focuses on developing feelings of goodwill, kindness, and warmth toward yourself and others.

Compassion Kindness Warmth Emotional Healing

Body Scan Meditation

A form of seated meditation emphasizing posture and breath

Zen Meditation, or Zazen, is a traditional Buddhist practice that emphasizes sitting posture, breath awareness, and letting thoughts pass by without attachment.

Posture Breath Non-Attachment Tradition
