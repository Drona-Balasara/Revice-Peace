Frequently Asked Questions

Hover over questions to see answers.

How do I create an account?

?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the login page, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start your wellness journey in minutes.

What if I forget my password?

?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.

How often should I journal?

?

We recommend journaling daily for best results, but even a few times a week can be beneficial. The key is consistency and being honest with yourself.

Are binaural beats safe to use?

?

Yes, binaural beats are generally safe. However, if you have epilepsy or are prone to seizures, consult with a healthcare professional before use.

Can I use Revive Peace offline?

?

Most features work offline, including journaling and some games. However, the AI chatbot requires an internet connection to function.

How do I export my journal entries?

?

Currently, journal entries are stored locally on your device. We're working on export features for future updates.

What age groups is Revive Peace suitable for?

?

Revive Peace is designed for adults and teenagers (13+). For younger users, parental guidance is recommended.

How do I change my account settings?

?

Account settings can be accessed through your profile menu. You can update your email, password, and preferences there.
